About

2020-04-02

As a lead consultant at a Microsoft Gold Partner I regularly encounter challenges within the Power Platform domain. The Power Platform and its capabilities evolve rapidly, and the knowledge required to utilize the latest functionality keeps growing. Microsoft defines the Power Platform as:

“the low-code platform that spans Office 365, Azure, Dynamics 365, and standalone applications.”

Power Platform

My focus for this blog is primarily on Dynamics 365, Flow and PowerApps.

As I always enjoy reading other blogs about how to use new functionality - with all its possible limitations, and how to overcome them - this blog is my view on the latest developments of the Power Platform.

Enjoy!
